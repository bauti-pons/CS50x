About Us

Your trusted partner in providing accurate and reliable currency exchange solutions

We Are Currencity

Welcome to Currencity, your go-to platform for accurate and reliable currency exchange solutions. As a final project for the CS50 HarvardX course, Currencity represents the culmination of extensive learning and dedication. Designed to cater to both novice and experienced users, our web app offers real-time currency conversion, historical exchange rate data, and an upcoming online community for financial discussions and networking.

At Currencity, our mission is to provide seamless and efficient currency exchange services, empowering users with the tools and information they need to make informed financial decisions. Join us on this journey as we continually enhance our features and strive to deliver the best user experience possible. Thank you for being a part of Currencity.
